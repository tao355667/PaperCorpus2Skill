# Instruction Tuning for Domain-Specific Writing Assistance

## Abstract
Instruction tuning is essential for adapting language models to domain-specific writing tasks.

## Introduction
Existing language models can be sensitive to vague prompts and domain shift. Modeling domain-specific instructions may improve response consistency.

## Method
The model is tuned on structured instruction-response pairs from academic writing tasks. A preference objective encourages concise and evidence-aware revisions.

## Experiments
Experiments report human preference scores and automatic style consistency metrics under in-domain and cross-domain protocols.

## Discussion
Instruction tuning is described as motivation, mechanism, and evaluation target across the paper.
