# Temporal Modeling for rPPG Signal Recovery

## Abstract
Temporal modeling is essential for recovering pulse-related signals from facial videos.

## Introduction
Existing rPPG methods can be sensitive to illumination variation and short-term motion. Modeling long-range temporal dependencies may improve signal consistency.

## Method
The model aggregates temporal cues across video segments. A temporal consistency loss encourages stable physiological signal recovery.

## Experiments
Experiments report heart rate error and waveform similarity under intra-dataset and cross-dataset protocols.

## Discussion
Temporal modeling is described as motivation, mechanism, and evaluation target across the paper.
